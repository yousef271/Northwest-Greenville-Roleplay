About Us

NorthWest Greenville Roleplay is a community-driven roleplay server on Discord. We provide a fun and engaging environment for roleplaying enthusiasts with various scenarios and events.

Server Rules

  • 1. No Swearing
  • 2. No Bullying
  • 3. No Spamming
  • 4. Be respectful to everyone
  • 5. No mass ping spam
  • 6. Must be 13+
  • 7. No Discord invites
  • 8. Follow Discord's Terms of Service

Public Services

  • Wisconsin State Patrol
  • Outagamie County Sheriff's Office
  • Fox Valley Metro Police Department
  • National Park Service
  • Greenville Fire Department

In-Game Rules

  • Yield to emergency vehicles.
  • Request voids from staff if necessary.
  • Wait 15 minutes before committing another crime.
  • Follow laws during peacetime.
  • Priority scenes must be announced by staff.
  • Respect all players and scenes.
